Greeley Health Fair

In April 16th 2022 the 9news organization will be hosting a health fair
where the people of Greeley will be able to get discounted and free screenings.
Ask for what the prices are for each screen or check online to see the prices for the
screenings that cost.

El 16th de Abril. El organizacion de 9news vamos a tener una feria de salud.
